About the Project

Wayfarers Guild is a third year university project created within a team of ten where I served as both a programmer and designer. Tasked with delivering the realms most important cargo, Wayfarers are flung into extradimensional spaces where they use their Crystal to find a path through the mist and to the portal awaiting them while evading the eldritch horrors hunting them. The game features LAN multiplayer, an intricate physics system (to facilitate 3D airship movement and rotation while keeping the players stationary), and A* pathfinding implemented in 3D for enemy AI.

Key Contributions

  • Game Design Document: Created the initial GDD when pitching the game, and maintained it throughout development.
  • Design Work: Defined design pillars, unique selling points, gameplay mechanics, and player experience.
  • Implemented Physics Plugin: Implemented and modified a physics plugin to allow it to work correctly with networking.
  • Networked Multiplayer: Used Netcode for Game Objects to add LAN multiplayer functionality.
  • Created A* Pathfinding in 3D: Used the theory for the A* pathfinding algorithm and implemented it in 3D for our enemy AI.
