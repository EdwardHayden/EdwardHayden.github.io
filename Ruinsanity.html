About the Project

Ruinsanity is a yearlong project I created with six other developers made in the Unreal Game Engine. You are an explorer tasked with delving into the depths of a long-forgotten temple on a distant peninsula and looting it for its treasures. Navigate 3D puzzles with the help of your time-bending amulet, peering into the past while keeping your sanity intact. Ruinsanity features a dual texture shader mechanic that the player uses to complete puzzles swapping textures and meshes of the world around them.

Key Contributions

  • Dual Texture Shader Mechanic: Created a mechanic to dynamically swap textures and meshes in a radius around the player using Unreal Shader Graphs.
  • Custom Camera System: Used Unreals Blueprints to implement a custom camera system that follows the player at the nearest point along a rail.
  • QA Lead: Ran the QA for the project, making questionnaires, and organising/recording data on playtesting.
