Infernal Link

2023

Background info

Infernal Link was my first full group project in university, it was also my first full game I created in Unity. I worked in a group of five, composed of four programmers, and one artist. We had six months to create a game based off some game jam style prompts that we were given. Most of this project for me was learning Unity and C# along with how to work effectively in a group dynamic, but it built a good foundation for my knowledge of the game engine for future projects.

About the game

Infernal Link is a splitscreen co-op, top-down, hack and slash game where you play as a Demon and a Demon Hunter bound together by a spectral chain who have to fight their way through waves of enemies to reach, and close, a portal to hell. The game features a diegetic combined health bar in the form of a physics-based chain that increases or decreases in length depending on the players health. The chain drags along the floor and can be wrapped around pillars and enemies, and the players have abilities that can utilise the chain to damage enemies and heal themselves.

Skills Gained

This project was my first time working in Unity with C# so I mainly focused on getting the basics down. Early on in production I focused on the enemy AI logic and then later moved onto working more generally for other aspects of the game.

  • Basics of Unity Game Engine
  • Basics of C#
  • Simple AI Implementation in Unity
  • Experience Working with a 3D Asset Pipeline
  • Basics of the Fungus Plugin for Unity
