Rainfall

2024

Background info

Rainfall is a project that I worked on as part of a level design module in university. During the module we developed design specific skills such as level design and construction, level pacing, the importance of audio and lighting in level design, and the aid of modular kits to aid in level design.

About the game

Rainfall is an atmospheric walking simulator that aims to elicit an emotional response through the use of visual and audio effects via a dynamic weather system.

Skills Gained

As this was my first time working with Unreal, some time was spent learning the new engine. However, due to having prior knowledge of Unity, the process was made a lot easier. Unreal's Blueprint system was very intuitive to learn with its block coding and was surpisingly easy to program more complicated mechanics.
