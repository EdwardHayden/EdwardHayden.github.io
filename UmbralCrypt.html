Umbral Crypt

2024

About the Project

Umbral Crypt is a two week game jam created in a three person team. You play as an alchemist collecting shattered pieces of a stone tablet used to open the way to the Philosopher's Stone, all while avoiding whatever lurks in the shadows. The game features a unique drawing mechanic where players use chalk to sketch runes into the 3D environment and depending on the rune they drew, a corresponding effect will trigger such as locking/unlocking a door or stunning the monster.

Key Contributions

  • Game Design Document: Created the GDD outlining design pillars, player experience, and gameplay systems.
  • Drawing in 3D: Created a mechanic allowing the player to draw using their cursor on the surrounding 3D environment.
  • Rune Recognition: Researched and implemented a 2D shape detection algorithm from an academic paper into Unity.
  • 3D Art: Modeled and textured assets for the game in Blender alongside another team member.
