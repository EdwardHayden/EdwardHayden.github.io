Runeguard 1

Explore a shadow-filled dungeon, home to animated suits of armour in this top down, 2D pixel art, turn-based, stamina mechanic prototype. With only a limited amount of stamina available each turn, you must divide it up between movement and actions to fight off the animated suits of armour.

Runeguard 2

Explore a shadown-filled dungeon, plunder hidden treasure, avoid traps, and find your way to the lever to open up the pathway to an evil paladin, all the while fending off his animated suits of armour lurking in the darkness in this top down, 2D pixel art, adventure game demo using a unique joint-stamina turn-based system.

Infernal Link

Set in a world where demons are bound to the mortal realm through ritualistic chains, a Demon Hunter and his unwilling ally must fight through hordes of evil cultists to close the portal to hell.

Ribbit Romance: Vol VII

Once upon a time, in a land not so far away (Slough), a Prince wanders into a tower in search of a princess only to find himself cursed. Turned into a frog and desperate for a true love kiss, his current tactic, accuracy by volume of fire, kissing anything that consents.

Umbral Crypt

As a member of the Parisian Chapter of Alchemists, you uncovered a lead into the whereabouts of the famed Philosopher’s Stone, bringing you to the Catacombs under the city where a dark presence lurks. Use your chalk to draw runes and transmutation circles to aid in your exploration while evading what hides in the shadows.

Ruinsanity

Explore a long-forgotten temple, navigate ancient 3D puzzles with the help of your time-bending amulet, peering into the past while keeping your sanity intact.

Rainfall

Traverse a large custom landscape with a dynamic weather system in this atmospheric walking simulator. As the player is pushed along by a gust of wind, experience an emotional journey shaped by the weather and soundscape around you.

Bardcore Rock

Survive an endless onslaught of goblins in this endless runner game where you are able to use your magically imbued keytar to fend off the oncoming horde. Bardcore Rock utalises two custom built game controllers of a Keytar and Dance Pad to immerse the player in the dungeon.
