Game Development Portfolio

Hello, I am currently in my third and final year of studying Games Development: Programming at Falmouth University. I started my game development journey using Godot with GD Script, and then switched to primarily using Unity and C#, whilst also gaining experience in Unreal Engine, and Unreal's Blueprint system. Prior to university I primarily programmed in Python and as such have around 5 years of experience using it (which is also why I initially liked Godot, due to the similarities between Python and GD Script).

I've worked in teams of up to 10 people, all with varying disciplines, and in more recent projects over the last two years, have taken on the responsibilities of overseeing our Agile workflow, organising meetings, and ensuring good communication within my team.

Runeguard 1

Explore a shadow-filled dungeon, home to animated suits of armour in this top down, 2D pixel art, turn-based, stamina mechanic prototype. With only a limited amount of stamina available each turn, you must divide it up between movement and actions to fight off the animated suits of armour.

Runeguard 2

Explore a shadown-filled dungeon, plunder hidden treasure, avoid traps, and find your way to the lever to open up the pathway to an evil paladin, all the while fending off his animated suits of armour lurking in the darkness in this top down, 2D pixel art, adventure game demo using a unique joint-stamina turn-based system.
